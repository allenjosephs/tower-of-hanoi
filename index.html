How to Play

Objective

Move all blocks from the left-hand tower to the right-hand tower

Rules

  1. Only one block may be moved at a time
  2. At no time can a larger block be placed on top of a smaller block

Gameplay

  1. Choose number of blocks
  2. Click "Start Game"
  3. Move blocks by dragging from one tower to another
  4. Drop blocks anywhere inside a valid 'drop zone'
  5. Click "Reset Game" to start over
